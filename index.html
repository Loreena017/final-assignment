Hi this is Lore!

Hi I am Loreena!

I am currently a GBDA student at the University of Waterloo, where I focus on game design, interactive media, and digital arts. I enjoy creating immersive experiences that invite players and users to explore, interact, and feel, while exploring how design can tell engaging and meaningful stories. My work spans game prototypes, 3D models, UX/UI designs, and experimental digital projects, each reflecting my curiosity and dedication to both form and function. I am passionate about combining creativity and technology to solve problems, craft memorable experiences, and push the boundaries of what digital media can achieve. Through this portfolio, I hope to share not only the skills I’ve developed but also my approach to storytelling, interactivity, and the playful experimentation that drives my work.

My Works

Currently pursuing my creative path, I’ve been drawn to art for as long as I can remember. I’m fascinated by people—their expressions, gestures, and the quiet stories they carry. Portraits and character studies allow me to explore these subtleties, while photography gives me a way to capture moments and places that feel alive. I chase light, shadow, and fleeting glimpses of emotion, preserving them so I can return to the feeling long after the moment has passed. Beyond technique, my work is an attempt to connect—with the world, with others, and with the fragments of life that often go unnoticed. Each drawing, each photograph, becomes a space where time pauses, stories linger, and emotions resurface, inviting both me and the viewer to look closer, feel deeper, and remember.
