Hi I am Loreena!

I am currently a GBDA student at the University of Waterloo, where I focus on game design, interactive media, and digital arts. I enjoy creating immersive experiences and exploring how design can tell engaging stories. This portfolio showcases my projects, including game prototypes, 3D models, and UX/UI designs. I am passionate about combining creativity and technology to solve problems and deliver unique digital experiences.

My works

Currently pursuing my creative path, I’ve been drawn to art for as long as I can remember. I’m fascinated by people—characters, portraits, and the quiet stories held in their expressions. Photography is a natural extension of that curiosity. I’m always chasing moments and places that feel alive, capturing them so I can return to the emotion and atmosphere long after they’ve passed. Through both mediums, I try to hold onto fleeting fragments of life and let them speak again.
